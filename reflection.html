I’m starting with the
man in the mirror

So how's it been?

Prior to attending university, I was working for a web agency in Bedford for 2 years and developed a fairly comprehensive skillset which made this initial term feel fairly slow; it did however offer me something different to what I was used to. During my two years industry, I created a host of products from email marketing templates to fully fledged websites for a range of companies including Epson UK/EU, Thomson Reuters and Acal BFI. A lot of the products I was developing were already designed for me and I had the task of then creating the final piece of work. Generally, I was using HTML5, CSS, Javascript and PHP while integrating pre-processors such a HAML, LESS and SASS and Git for version control. While my skillset was already built up I found the first term of CSY1018 a good way to take myself back to the basics and remember the small things that I had forgotten. Not only did I get to go back to basics I also had the opportunity to design a lot more products. Often, I’d only find myself designing web pages for the occasional piece of freelance work so having the couple of sessions where it was primarily design based was a great experience for myself.

Having spoken to the teaching assistants about what is to come, I’m very excited for the remaining time I have doing web development.
